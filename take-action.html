Help SAFE Gardening by donating to our organization. Funds are used to provide education regarding allergy friendly plants and to purchase allergy friendly plants to be planted to improve the environment.

The Society for Allergy Friendly Environmental (SAFE) Gardening is registered as a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. Contributions to SAFE Gardening are tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law. SAFE Gardening's tax identification number is 46-2869063.

Plant Allergy Friendly Plants

Take the opportunity to plant allergy friendly plants when redoing your landscaping or putting in a new landscape.

Plant milkweed and add your location to the "Milkweed Highway"

The purpose of the Milkweed Highway is to encourage people to plant and maintain new native milkweed plants that will feed and nurture vast numbers of butterflies. Milkweed plants have been disappearing in our environment which is threatening the species of butterflies that depend on them such as the Monarchs. Monarch butterflies cannot exist without the milkweed plant.

For more information, go to: Milkweed Highway

Ask local nurseries to stock allergy friendly plants

Local nurseries are not always aware that they may have plants that help to reduce the impact of allergy and asthma. Help us make nurseries aware that allergy friendly plants help the environment and encourage them to have allergy plants available and labeled appropriately.
